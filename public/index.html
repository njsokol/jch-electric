KC Electric is a family owned and operated company that has provided outstanding electrical contracting services in the Dallas Metroplex for over 25 years. We are dedicated to providing skilled craftsmanship, exceptional service and competitive pricing to our clients.


Our range of experience covers many facets of both commercial and industrial construction including: retail shopping, corporate offices, warehouses and government buildings. Our specialty is custom high end restaurants. We work on one of a kind designs as well as chain concepts with Brinker, Darden, Front Burner, IHOP and MiCocina to name a few.


At KC Electric, we strive to exceed the expectations of every General Contractor, Owner, Designer and Architect who we work with. We understand the importance of deadlines and make all efforts to complete each project on time and on budget. KC Electric employs a staff of electricians with combined experience of over 100 years in the electrical trade.


We take great pride in our work and look forward to the opportunity of doing business with you and your company.
